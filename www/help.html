Polygon Calculator

Use this calculator to calculate the Area and the Perimeter of commons regular
polygons (Triangle, Square, ..., Decagon).

To use the calculator :

  1. Select the polygon name,
  2. Enter the length of the side,
  3. Choose the Units in which the area and the perimeter will be calculated",
    (Note: this doen't influence on the computation, it is just for convenience.)
  4. Press the button Calculate to get the result.


The results of computation are displayed in", tags$b("Results panel.

Note : By default when the App loads, the side is set to 1 and the polygon is Triangle.
